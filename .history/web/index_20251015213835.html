About This System

Technology Behind the Safety

Our drowsiness detection system uses advanced computer vision techniques to monitor driver alertness in real-time. By analyzing facial landmarks and eye movements, we can detect signs of fatigue before they become dangerous.

Why It Matters

Drowsy driving is responsible for thousands of accidents each year. This system provides an extra layer of safety by alerting drivers when they show signs of drowsiness, potentially saving lives.
